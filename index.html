OUR SERVICES

Adult Specialists & Allied Health

We provide quality and affordable healthcare at over 60 General Practitioners (GP) medical clinics islandwide under the Healthway Medical and Silver Cross brand names. The Group is aligned with the Ministry of Health’s (MOH) primary focus to keep Singaporeans healthy by offering preventive care services. Our services include general medical consultations, management of chronic medical conditions, health screening packages and preventive medicine.
